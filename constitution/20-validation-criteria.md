# Part IV – Research

# 20. Validation Criteria

## 20.1 Purpose

Validation ensures engineering decisions are reliable before adoption.

## 20.2 Validation Requirements

Validation must include appropriate:

- Testing.
- Simulation.
- Benchmarking.
- Review.

## 20.3 Evidence Standard

Claims must be supported by measurable evidence.

## 20.4 Continuous Validation

All major improvements require repeated validation after implementation.
