# Part II – Governance

# 10. Change Management

## 10.1 Purpose

Change management controls how Hermes OS evolves.

## 10.2 Change Principles

Changes must be:

- Documented.
- Reviewed.
- Tested.
- Validated.

## 10.3 Change Categories

Changes include:

- Architecture changes.
- Feature changes.
- Infrastructure changes.
- Process improvements.

## 10.4 Change Validation

No significant change is accepted without evidence supporting the improvement.
