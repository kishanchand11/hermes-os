# Part III – Planning

# 14. Risk Register

## 14.1 Purpose

The risk register identifies engineering, operational, security, and execution risks affecting Hermes OS.

## 14.2 Risk Categories

- Architecture risk.
- Technology risk.
- Security risk.
- Performance risk.
- Operational risk.

## 14.3 Risk Management

Risks must be identified, analyzed, mitigated, monitored, and documented.

## 14.4 Arbitrage System Risks

Important risks include:

- Gas cost changes.
- Liquidity changes.
- Slippage.
- Failed execution.
- Smart contract risk.
- Infrastructure failures.
