# Part II – Governance

# 08. Decision Authority

## 8.1 Purpose

Decision authority defines who and what controls engineering decisions.

## 8.2 Technical Decisions

Technical decisions must be based on research, testing, and measurable evidence.

## 8.3 Architecture Decisions

Architecture decisions require documented rationale and trade-off analysis.

## 8.4 Change Approval

Significant changes require review before adoption.

## 8.5 Decision Records

Important decisions must be preserved through Architecture Decision Records.
