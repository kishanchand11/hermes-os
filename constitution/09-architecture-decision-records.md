# Part II – Governance

# 09. Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

## 9.1 Purpose

Architecture Decision Records preserve important engineering choices and their reasoning.

## 9.2 ADR Requirements

Every major architectural decision must document:

- Context.
- Alternatives considered.
- Decision.
- Trade-offs.
- Consequences.

## 9.3 ADR Lifecycle

ADRs are created, reviewed, accepted, superseded, and archived.

## 9.4 Importance

ADRs maintain institutional knowledge and prevent repeated analysis of previously solved problems.
