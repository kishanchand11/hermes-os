# Part III – Planning

# 11. Product Objectives

## 11.1 Primary Objective

Build a validated Base DEX arbitrage system using disciplined engineering practices.

## 11.2 Engineering Objectives

The system must provide:

- Reliable market analysis.
- Opportunity detection.
- Profitability evaluation.
- Controlled execution.
- Continuous improvement.

## 11.3 Quality Objective

The product must prioritize correctness, observability, and maintainability.
