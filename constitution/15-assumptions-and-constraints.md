# Part III – Planning

# 15. Assumptions and Constraints

## 15.1 Purpose

This document defines assumptions and fixed constraints governing Hermes OS.

## 15.2 Locked Constraints

- Base blockchain only.
- DEX only.
- Localhost-first operation.
- DevOps-first workflow.

## 15.3 Engineering Assumptions

Assumptions must be explicitly documented and validated as the project evolves.

## 15.4 Constraint Management

Constraints may only change through governance review and documented decisions.
