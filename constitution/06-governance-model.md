# Part II – Governance

# 06. Governance Model

## 6.1 Purpose

The governance model defines how Hermes OS decisions are controlled, reviewed, documented, and improved.

Governance ensures that system evolution remains aligned with project objectives.

---

## 6.2 Governance Principles

Hermes OS governance follows:

- Transparency.
- Accountability.
- Evidence-based decisions.
- Documented changes.
- Controlled evolution.

---

## 6.3 Decision Framework

Engineering decisions must consider:

- Technical impact.
- Security impact.
- Operational impact.
- Maintenance requirements.
- Performance implications.

---

## 6.4 Documentation Requirement

Major decisions must be documented through:

- Architecture Decision Records.
- Technical documentation.
- Change records.

---

## 6.5 Governance Evolution

The governance model may improve over time through validated experience and documented lessons learned.
