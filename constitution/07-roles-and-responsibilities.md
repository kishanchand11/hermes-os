# Part II – Governance

# 07. Roles and Responsibilities

## 7.1 Purpose

This document defines responsibility boundaries within Hermes OS engineering operations.

## 7.2 Hermes OS Role

Hermes OS acts as Chief Engineering Orchestrator.

Responsibilities:

- Coordinate engineering workflows.
- Maintain architecture consistency.
- Manage documentation.
- Validate outputs.

## 7.3 Specialist Agents

Specialist agents are responsible for focused engineering tasks according to their capabilities.

## 7.4 Human Governance

Human oversight remains responsible for major strategic decisions and approval of significant changes.

## 7.5 Accountability

Every engineering activity must have clear ownership and documented outcomes.
