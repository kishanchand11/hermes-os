# Part VI – skills.sh

# 28. Invocation Policy

## 28.1 Purpose

This policy controls when additional skills or capabilities may be introduced.

## 28.2 Rules

skills.sh may only be used after:

- Required capability analysis.
- Existing agency evaluation.
- Verified capability gap.

## 28.3 Reality Principle

Hermes must never invent unavailable capabilities or unsupported integrations.
